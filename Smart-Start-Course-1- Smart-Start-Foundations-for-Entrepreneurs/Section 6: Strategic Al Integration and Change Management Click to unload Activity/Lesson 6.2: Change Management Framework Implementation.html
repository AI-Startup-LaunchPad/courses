Resistance Management and Mitigation

Resistance to AI adoption is natural and predictable. Effective change management anticipates resistance patterns and provides targeted interventions to address specific concerns and barriers.

High Resistance
Active Opposition

Vocal criticism, spreading negative sentiment, refusing to participate in training

Intervention: One-on-one meetings, address specific concerns, involve in solution design

Medium Resistance
Passive Resistance

Minimal participation, skeptical questions, slow adoption of new processes

Intervention: Peer mentoring, success story sharing, gradual exposure

Low Resistance
Cautious Acceptance

Willing to try but needs reassurance, asks clarifying questions, seeks support

Intervention: Clear communication, training support, regular check-ins

Champion Potential
Early Adopters

Enthusiastic about AI, quick to learn, willing to help others

Strategy: Develop as champions, peer mentors, and success story examples
